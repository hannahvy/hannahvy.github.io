about me



Hi! I'm a student studying Computer Science at Florida International University. I'm also pursuing a certificate in User Experience Design at Cornell University.

My passion for design, however, began long before my educational endeavors. I remember doodling characters and concepts as soon as I could hold a pencil, filling sheets of paper to the brim. I've always held creativity close to my heart. That didn't change when I became exposed to computer science. My IDE became my canvas; the idea of creating something new and appreciated by others continues to excites me.

In 2018, when I was a rising high school senior, I gained my first industry experience as a Software Development intern at SAP North America in Newtown Square, PA. My main role on the team was Frontend Development, in which I applied my HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js skills to build a user dashboard. I also spent the following summer as a #SAPien, collaborating with the Mission-Critical Support team to seek solutions for clients with software problems.

I am always learning new things about software development and UI/UX design and continually looking for opportunities to develop my skills. Please feel free to chat with me at hnguy073@fiu.edu
