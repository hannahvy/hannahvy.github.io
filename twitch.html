ViewerMap Twitch Extension

Preface

In September 2019, I was invited to compete in the inaugural Amazon Games x Twitch Hackathon, held by 1,000 Dreams Fund at Yale University in New Haven, Connecticut. This was my first ever hackathon event. THE CHALLENGE The hackathon organizers, mentors, and volunteer professionals (including Amazon Games and Twitch developers) challenged us to create a Twitch extension using the Twitch Developer Rig. This tool allows developers to create extensions for the popular streaming site effectively and locally. The judging criteria consisted of: Originality/Creativity Complexity and Code Elegance Simplicity Market Potential/Growth Completion I was placed on a team of four. With the hackathon being a single-day event, we were given only six hours to take on the challenge and finish a Minimum Viable Product (MVP). THE USERS + AUDIENCE The purpose of an extension is to further connect and engage the audience with the primary streamer. To form a well-rounded concept for our project, we decided to reflect on the values of Twitch itself.
